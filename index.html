Strategy

Clarity in the Chaos

DragonFly is a proprietary quantitative trading system. Mathematically defined entries. Algorithmically adjusted position sizing. Systematic risk parameters. Full automation, full precision — 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

01

Mean Reversion

Multi-ladder approach that scales into positions as price deviates from calculated entries. Each layer builds a stronger weighted average — mathematically positioned for recovery.

02

Algorithmic Sizing

Position sizes are algorithmically adjusted across every ladder to optimize risk-to-reward at each level of exposure. Calculated, not guessed. Every time.

03

Systematic Exit

Profit targets are mathematically defined. When the market reverts, the position closes. Volatility becomes the advantage — captured with precision.
